JobFlow Autofill — Stop Re-Typing, Start Applying

Tired of filling out the same job application forms over and over? JobFlow Autofill is a Chrome extension that saves your profile once and auto-fills job applications across all major applicant tracking systems. Apply faster, track smarter, and keep your data private.

🔹 WHAT IT DOES

JobFlow Autofill detects job application forms on the websites you visit and fills them in with your saved profile data — instantly. No more typing your name, email, phone number, work history, and education into every single application. Save your information once, apply everywhere.

The extension automatically recognizes form fields including:
• Personal information (name, email, phone, address)
• Work experience (company, title, dates, descriptions)
• Education (school, degree, field of study, graduation date)
• Skills and certifications
• LinkedIn URL, portfolio, and website links
• Common screening questions (work authorization, salary expectations, start date)

🔹 KEY FEATURES

⚡ One-Click Autofill
Land on a job application, click the JobFlow icon, and watch your information fill in automatically. What used to take 15-20 minutes now takes seconds.

📋 Smart Field Detection
JobFlow uses intelligent pattern matching to identify form fields correctly — even on forms it has never seen before. Your phone number goes in the phone field, not the address field.

📊 Built-In Application Tracker
Every application you submit is automatically logged. View all your applications in a clean Kanban board, track pipeline stages (Applied → Interviewing → Offer), and never lose track of where you've applied.

🔔 Follow-Up Reminders
Set reminders to follow up with companies after applying. JobFlow notifies you when it's time to send that follow-up email, so opportunities don't slip through the cracks.

👤 Complete Profile Management
Store your full professional profile — work history, education, skills, certifications, and custom answers to common screening questions. Edit anytime, and your updates apply to all future applications.

📱 Optional Cloud Sync
Want to use JobFlow across multiple devices? Enable cloud sync to keep your profile and application history in sync. Prefer to stay local? That works too — sync is entirely optional.

🔹 PRIVACY & SECURITY — YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS

JobFlow is built local-first. Your personal information — name, address, work history, salary expectations — is stored on YOUR device, not on our servers.

🔒 Local-first architecture: Profile data is stored in your browser's local storage
🔒 No server required: The extension works completely offline, no account needed
🔒 Optional sync: Cloud sync is opt-in only, and you can delete your cloud data anytime
🔒 No data selling: We never sell, share, or monetize your personal information
🔒 Open approach: Your data is yours. Export it, delete it, or move it anytime

Unlike other autofill tools that upload your resume and personal data to third-party servers, JobFlow keeps everything on your machine by default. You are always in control.

🔹 FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes. JobFlow stores your data locally on your device by default. No data is sent to any server unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

Q: Does it work with LinkedIn Easy Apply?
A: JobFlow works best with full application forms on ATS platforms like Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, Ashby, SmartRecruiters, and iCIMS. LinkedIn Easy Apply uses a simplified flow that doesn't always expose standard form fields.

Q: Can I use it on multiple computers?
A: Yes — enable optional cloud sync to keep your profile and application history in sync across devices.

Q: Does it auto-submit applications?
A: No. JobFlow fills in your information but never submits an application without your review and approval. You always have the final say.

Q: What if a form field is filled incorrectly?
A: You can always edit any field after autofill. JobFlow also lets you configure field mappings for specific sites.
